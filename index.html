Emergence · AI · Markets · Mind

I study emergence through research, building, and investing. I want to deeply understand how intelligence, judgment, creativity, and markets form from underlying signals.

My work sits at the intersection of AI (deep learning & reinforcement learning), markets, and the mind. I build systems that surface latent structure—whether in EEG data, financial instruments, prediction markets, or human behavior—by treating intelligence as a dynamical process rather than a static trait.

I study the mind through the Tibetan Buddhist lineage of Kadampa, train technical rigor through modern AI, and apply both to real systems with real stakes. Previously, I've helped raise $220M+ across startups, built 0→1 products supporting a $15B revenue line, and worked as a strategic operator across venture, finance, and product.

Today, I focus on identifying generative attractors—the conditions under which clarity, insight, and alpha reliably emerge—and turning them into tools, frameworks, and living experiments.

I'm always teasing out new ideas, building in public, and happy to talk shop.
