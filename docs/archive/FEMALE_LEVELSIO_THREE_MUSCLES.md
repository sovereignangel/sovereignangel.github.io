- Armstrong: New UI component, analytics feature, bugfix
- Manifold: Dataset improvement, interface update, new market

**The Key:** 3+ ships per week = unstoppable momentum

**For you:**

- Public shipping = people see momentum (FOMO, want to be part of it)

**Network effect:**
- Week 1 ship → 5 people see it

## MUSCLE 2: REVENUE ASKS (3 per Day, Live with Rejection)

### What This Means

You ask for money **3 times per day, every day.**

Not soft asks. **Hard asks.**
- "Will you pay $X/month for this?"
- "Can I send you an invoice for $Y?"
- "Is this worth $Z to you?"

**The constraint:** You don't care if 90% say no. Your job is to improve the "yes" rate from 5% → 10% → 15%.

### Why This Works

**For you:**
- 3 asks/day × 5 days = 15 asks/week
- If conversion rate is 10%, that's 1.5 customers/week
- That's 6-8 customers/month
- That compounds to $30k+/month revenue in 12 months

**Psychology:**
- Most founders ask 0-1 times per day (shame + anxiety)
- You asking 3x = 3x the test data
- 3x test data = 3x faster learning
- Faster learning = better message, better conversion
- Better conversion = compound effect

**Levelsio proof:** He charges for his work immediately. No "beta tester" phase. No "free trial forever." He asks, tracks response, refines message.

### How to Master This

**Daily (non-negotiable):**

- [ ] 15 total asks

- [ ] Identify: Which persona responds most?

**Monthly:**
- [ ] Refine: Message based on data
- [ ] Test: New angle or new persona

- [ ] Celebrate: X% improvement or X new customers

## MUSCLE 3: PUBLIC NARRATIVE (Build in Public, Metrics Visible)

### What This Means

You tell the story of what you're building **as you build it**, with numbers visible.

Not:
- ❌ Hiding until you're "ready"
- ❌ Launching in stealth
- ❌ Keeping metrics private
- ❌ Only sharing wins

Yes:
- ✅ Weekly progress visible (shipped X, learned Y)
- ✅ Revenue transparent (made $Z/month, chasing $Z+1)
- ✅ Failures public (tried X, didn't work, here's why)
- ✅ Thesis evolving (thought Y, now thinking Z)

### Why This Works

**For you:**
- Public building = social proof (people see you shipping)
- Visible metrics = credibility (you're not BS-ing)
- Transparent failures = trust (you're honest, not a hack)
- Narrative pull = inbound (people want to be part of story)

**The compounding effect:**
- Week 1: 5 people see you ship

- Month 3: 100+ people anticipating your update
- Month 6: Inbound inquiries, warm intros, partnership offers
- Month 12: You're not asking anymore, people are asking to work with you

**Levelsio proof:** His entire moat is transparency. Revenue public. Projects public. Failures public. People trust him because he hides nothing.
